Our maintenance programs

3 OPTIONS

1.

You already own plants

We can take over their guaranteed maintenance.

2.

Purchase with guaranteed maintenance

We sell you the plants and their decorative containers and we provide

the weekly guaranteed maintenance.

3.

Lease with guaranteed maintenance

We lease you the plants and their decorative

containers and we provide the weekly guaranteed maintenance.

What are the differences between purchase or lease?

By leasing you avoid the initial expense of the purchase. Keep in mind

that when you lease, your monthly fee might be up to 50% higher than

if you purchased the plants and had us provide the regular guaranteed

maintenance.With both options, the service remains the same.
