What are the differences between purchasing or

leasing?

By leasing you avoid the initial expense of a purchase. Keep in mind that when you lease, your monthly fee might be up to 50% higher than if you purchased the plants and had us provide the regular guaranteed maintenance. With either option, our quality of service remains the same.
