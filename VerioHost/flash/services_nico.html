  our services

Before you decide to use Greencare, we will:

* Visit with you and evaluate your needs.
* Explain to you our services and programs.
* Suggest a design adapted to your space and budget.
* Provide you with a comprehensive proposal.

While using Greencare our services include:

* Delivery and installation of the plants and their decorative containers.

* Weekly visits by a friendly, uniformed and experienced professional horticulturist technician.

* Blooming rotation: if your plant collection includes bloomers (Orchids, Bromeliads...) we will rotate them on a regular basis.

* Plant guaranty: any serviced plant will be promptly replaced at no extra charge when falling below Greencare's high standard.

* Regular visits by management to ensure communication with customers and consistency in service.

* Plant maintenance: watering, feeding, pruning and trimming, dusting and polishing, pest control and all necessary actions to insure the beauty of your plants.
